Dexter the knife thrower

Dexter has been throwing knifes since he can remember. Over the years, He has reached a dexterity beyond imagination with those tools. Among other performances, you will glaze at him undress his assistant from eight meters away, just by throwing his knifes on the right spot of the clothes

El Diablo the fire tamer

Ask El Diablo about his gift and he will tell you that it is a curse. But for the people looking at his incredible fire dance, this is clearly not a curse. We are aware that you will try not to blink, to get every seconds of the show, but please, do blink otherwise the intense fire will dry your eyes!

The Weasley twins

The Weasley twins are probably the best magicians ever. Some of their tricks are so impressive that the magician community has gathered to try to solve the Weasley riddle, without any success. Some people say that no tricks can explain their show and conclude that the Weasley are real wizards...
